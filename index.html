About This App

MikroTik Tools is a powerful and intuitive Android application designed for network administrators and enthusiasts who work with MikroTik routers and devices. It provides a comprehensive suite of tools to monitor, configure, and troubleshoot your MikroTik network directly from your smartphone or tablet.

Whether you're managing a small home network or a complex corporate infrastructure, MikroTik Tools offers the functionality you need to stay in control.

Key Features

  • Device Discovery: Easily find and connect to MikroTik devices on your network.
  • Real-time Monitoring: Keep an eye on CPU usage, memory, traffic, and connected clients.
  • Configuration Management: Modify router settings, manage users, and configure interfaces.
  • Traffic Analysis: Visualize network traffic and identify bandwidth hogs.
  • Ping & Traceroute: Essential network diagnostic tools at your fingertips.
  • Secure Connection: Supports secure connections to your MikroTik devices.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Designed for ease of use on mobile devices.

About InternetMeter

InternetMeter is an essential iOS application designed to give you complete control and insight into your internet connection. With its intuitive interface, you can effortlessly monitor your real-time download and upload speeds, track daily and monthly data usage, and identify potential network bottlenecks.

Whether you're concerned about exceeding your data cap, troubleshooting slow internet, or simply curious about your network performance, InternetMeter provides accurate and easy-to-understand metrics right on your iPhone or iPad.

Key Features

  • Real-time Speed Monitoring: See your current download and upload speeds instantly.
  • Data Usage Tracking: Keep a detailed log of your daily, weekly, and monthly data consumption.
  • Historical Data: Review past performance and usage trends.
  • Customizable Alerts: Set notifications for data usage thresholds.
  • Network Information: View details about your connected Wi-Fi or cellular network.
  • Sleek & Intuitive UI: A user-friendly design optimized for iOS.
